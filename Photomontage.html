Katie's Photomontage.html

My photomontage is supposed to convey the idea that music makes the world a happier place. The two background images I used were old ones I found on my phone that I have taken during vacation. I chose to use the sea as the background because it represents peace. The bear is one my cousin gave me for my birthday because it represents my intrest in the color purple. The bear is supposed to be flying towards the happy side because of the music. The backgrounds are supposed to show how the bears emotions are changing from something negative to positive because it is listening to music. I chose to make my photomontage like this because listening to music is my hobby and it makes me happy when I'm upset. I used the subtract blend mode on the darker half of the background to make it look even more darker and sad, and I used the gradient tool to combine the 2 backgrounds. The only images I got online were the umbrella and the musical notes. I used the generative Ai feature to make the headphones on the bear. I used photoshop text to make the words and size them.
