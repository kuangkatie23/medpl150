For this photo It was taken at an empty art classroom at the West building of the school. I thought the drawing was nice because it was a person with two faces. I feel like if you picture that it would sound weird, but this person made it look nice. I feel like it looks cool and that the face is almost like the face of a horse. When I took the photo I noticed this one looks really dark, since it was pasted on the wall under a shelf, so I wanted to edit it to make it brighter so that people can see the details in this artwork. As I played around with the curves tool in Adobe Bridge I noticed that as I changed the colors the frame around the photo slowly became many shades of blue and pink. I thought this was really pretty so I kept it. Overall compared to the original the new one I editied is so much brighter so people can see it and it makes this persons art stand out more.
